Description             : Ashes of Annihilation is a 20-map WAD for Doom II.
                          Although it uses a vanilla visual style, this WAD
                          requires a source port compatible with UMAPINFO for
                          everything to work properly. MAPINFO support added
                          for Zandronum
                          
                           
                          
                          The entire WAD takes place in a remote area of the
                          city and is divided into three parts: the first
                          consists of technological bases kept isolated for
                          secrecy. 
                          
                          The second covers nearby industries that work with
                          the UAC, where the demonic invasion began. 
                          
                          The third takes place in a demon-corrupted area,
                          serving as a Doom II�textured counterpart to E4.
                          
                           
                          
                          For these maps, I experimented with shorter, less
                          detailed levels, as well as a different layout
                          compared to my previous WADs. This set also
                          includes a remake of a Bloodveil map.
